Artist Statement

My photographic work explores color as both atmosphere and structure. Each image isolates a dominant chromatic condition, allowing color to function not merely as description but as subject.

This portfolio is structured as an online publication — part archive, part exhibition space — where viewers can navigate the work through chromatic themes rather than chronological order.

Inspired by editorial layouts, luxury fashion minimalism, and digital platforms such as Pinterest and Figma, the site emphasizes rhythm, spacing, and visual cadence.
